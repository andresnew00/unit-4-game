Poker Collector

Instructions

You are given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cards. By clicking on a card you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total sore goes above the random number.

The value of each card is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the calues of each card.
